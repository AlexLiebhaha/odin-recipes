Basic Mashed Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of baking potatoes, peeled and cut into quarters
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 cup of milk
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Bring a post of salted water to a boil. Add the potatoes and cook until tender but still firm, should be aobut 15 minutes, then drain.
  2. In a small saucepan, heat butter and milk over low heat until the butter is melted. Using a potato masher, slowly blend the milk mixture into the potatoes until they are smooth and creamy. Season with salt and pepper to your taste.
